Gender gap in science achievement
On average across the OECD countries, performance of boys and girls in science is similar, but there are differences between countries in relative performance of boys and girls. In mathematics, in many countries boys performed better or there were negligible differences between genders, so the higher performance of girls in Saudi Arabia was quite unusual. In reading, girls outperformed boys almost everywhere but the difference was especially large in Saudi Arabia. In science, there is a similar number of countries with higher performance of girls and with higher performance of boys, but for many PISA 2018 participants performance of boys and girls is highly similar.
